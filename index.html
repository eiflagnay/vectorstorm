Tactical Briefing

1. Core Navigational Protocols

  • Vector Movement: Follows input with fluid inertia. Use momentum to "drift" around hostile void signatures.
  • The Multiplier: Destroying blocks and survival increases score. Pulse Cells boost multipliers instantly.

2. Kinetic Armor & Singularity Burst

Reaching multiples of 10 Armor charges triggers a Storm Overload:

  • Singularity (Charge-up): Violent vibration and a Gravity Well that pulls all nearby matter toward the core for 0.8s.
  • Supernova (Release): Massive purple shockwave vaporizes captured hostiles with immense screen feedback.

3. Modular Weapon Systems

The Prism Array enters the field after Level 10. Power tiers (1-5) are persistent throughout the run. Tier 5 triggers a limited-duration Max Power state.
